About

Understandable before trust.

ONCHYRA is a decentralised referral protocol built on Polygon. Every referral, every commission, every level — all verifiable on-chain. No closed doors, no hidden mechanics, no promises you can't check.

Features

Built different.

Every layer of the protocol is designed for transparency, accountability, and long-term value.

L1

Multi-Level Referrals

Earn commissions across three referral levels — L1 (10%), L2 (4%), L3 (2%) — automatically distributed on-chain.

V

Verifiable History

Every commission, every withdrawal, every transfer is recorded on-chain and visible in real time. No blind spots.

S

Secure Wallets

Integrated deposit wallet system. Manage earnings, track balance, and withdraw — all within the protocol.

L

Live Leaderboard

See where you rank. Top earners, team builders, and most active referrers — all updated in real time.

P

Instant Payouts

Commissions are credited the moment a referral completes a qualifying action. No delay, no manual processing.

N

Polygon Native

Deployed on Polygon for fast, low-cost transactions. Full ERC-20 compatibility with AURA token integration.

How It Works

Three steps to start earning.

Join the protocol, share your referral link, and earn commissions as your network grows.

1

Create Account

2

Get your unique referral link and share it. Your downline is tracked automatically — no manual work.

3

Earn Commissions

Earn from three levels of referrals. L1, L2, L3 — all paid out on-chain, instantly, transparently.

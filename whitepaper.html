1. Introduction

Onchyra Network is a decentralized asset distribution protocol built for the mobile-first generation. Our mission is to simplify the complex world of blockchain mining through a user-friendly cloud-simulation model.

The ecosystem is designed to reward early adopters who contribute to network growth, ensuring a wide and fair distribution of the ONC token supply before the official mainnet launch.

2. Referral Incentives

Onchyra rewards users for expanding the network. These are Fixed One-Time Rewards credited to your wallet balance immediately upon a successful new registration within your referral tree.

Level 1 (Direct)

Level 2 (Tier 2)

*Signup rewards are credited once per unique user verified by the system. No recurring boosts are applied.

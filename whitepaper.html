The ONCHYRA Vision

Phase 2: Transitioning from Participation to Active Mining Economy.

Abstract

ONCHYRA is a specialized digital ecosystem built on the principles of Proof of Activity (PoA). Unlike traditional networks that require massive energy, ONCHYRA utilizes community engagement as its primary engine for growth.

We are building a bridge between early-stage participation and long-term blockchain utility.

Mining Mechanics

In Phase 2, the network officially transitions to a decentralized distribution model. Every 24 hours, users can initiate a 'Mining Cycle' through the secure portal.

To ensure a genuine community, ONCHYRA employs multi-layer bot detection. Any attempt to automate mining through scripts or multiple accounts will result in a Permanent Node Blacklist.
